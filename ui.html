Description of each frame type

# Portfolio

This is your go-to image for showcasing your work on Behance. It's optimized with a width of 1400 pixels, while the height can expand infinitely. With this frame, your portfolio presentation will be sleek, professional, and captivating. It's the perfect way to make a lasting impression and let your work shine on the platform.

# Cover Image

The Cover Image is your opportunity to infuse creativity and personalization into your portfolio. By simply clicking on the suggested dimension, a perfectly sized frame will be created, allowing you to unleash your artistic prowess.

# Profile Banner

Add a touch of creativity and personalization to your portfolio with a stunning banner. Click on the suggested dimension, and voila! A frame of that size will be created, empowering you to unleash your artistic prowess.

# Avatar

Your avatar represents your unique identity and personality. With a simple click on the recommended dimension, a perfectly sized frame will be generated, enabling you to showcase your avatar in all its glory.
